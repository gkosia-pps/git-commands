Semantic versioning:
    
    major.minor.patch

    major: if major number increases means that the change is incompatiple with older versions
           foundational changes

           releasing 2.0.0 means is not compatiple with 1.0.0

    minor: adding functionality
           is backwotk compatiple

           releasing 0.2.0 is compatible with 0.1.0 (is not completly different)

    patch: fix a bag or release something that is not fully production ready
